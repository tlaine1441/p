Ally.io

Ally.io is an anonymous, virtual buddy to help connect survivors of sexual assault with appropriate support resources via a conversation-like interface. This project tries to tackle the world-wide issue of connecting survivors of sexual assault with resources and field professionals to support them. This app focuses primarily on user experience and a clean, intuitive UI to help user in as few clicks as possible, while still maintaining an aura of anonymity.

  • Date: May 2017
  • Service: Web Development

Prem HR

My team and I were approached by SesientHR in Boulder, Colorado to build a Slack bot for them named Prem. Prem is a bot that helps employees cope with stressful emotions in the workplace. When employees face an emotionally challenging situation in the workplace (ex. I had a meeting that was incredibly infuriating), they don't have a mechanism for coping with the problem. Bring a psychotherapy staple - Cognitive Behavioral Therapy - to employees when they need it. Prem also checks in with employees and utilizes sentiment analysis to gauge and track emotions week to week.

  • Date: April 2017
  • Service: Bot Development
